Here's the thing...

I have always had more passions than I could keep up with, but that never stopped me from trying. I entered college as a Graphic Designer and will leave as a Web Designer and Developer who still loves graphic design and even studio art projects.

I'm Natalie, a flamboyant web designer/front-end developer, graphic designer, and studio artist with a keen eye for exciting and unique designs.
I thrive on creating a world within a project that I can develop from start to finish.

My journey began when I attended tech school for graphic design during my junior and senior high school years. Since then, I've had the privilege of studying web design and even interning at my university's web services as a junior web developer. Seeing what front-end development involves has only made me a better digital designer.
My creativity is sparked by a mix of caffeine, an overactive imagination, and a love for the mundane process of fixing code and design errors. Whether it's a team project, personal pursuit, or campaign all my own, I approach every situation with a contagiously positive attitude and blend innovation, dedication, and old-fashioned "stick-to-itiveness" to create designs that clients will never forget.

Reach out any time—I'm open and ready to work with you or hear a good web development joke.
